Description

Folklore is the eighth studio album by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift, released during the COVID-19 pandemic as a "surprise album".

The album marks a departure from Swift's previous pop sound, embracing indie folk and alternative rock with subdued instrumentation and wistful melodies. Lyrically, it features fictional narratives and character studies, drawing inspiration from her imagination during isolation.
